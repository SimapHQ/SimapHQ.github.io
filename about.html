What is Simap?

Simap is a tool for tracking home storage inventory. If you're trying to accumulate food storage for a rainy day, or if you're just tired of forgetting what you already have at home, Simap can help you.

Simap is an acronym for Storage Inventory Management And Planning. With Simap, you can track how much you currently have of any item in your home. Next time you see something on sale at the grocery store, you won't need to remember if you needed more or not, just check Simap.

Simap is more than just an inventory tracker, it can also help you meet your goals for home storage. For each item, you can tell Simap how much you use on a regular basis. Then, once you put in your goal and family size, Simap can show you your progress.
